Graphic Design

Why Graphic Design Interests Me

The past few years have been influential in my development of design skills. I started with simple illustrations in Adobe Illustrator like the house and Pepsi can, but transitioned to integrating Photoshop into the designs. Design, as a whole, opened my eyes to the intricacies of everyday objects and improved my detail-oriented mind. Every little detail counts and needs to be made so that the design works well.

Slice Instagram Post

The composition on the right was designed for DES 325. I focused on implementing the scissors into the word "slice" by creating the illusion that the scissors are slicing through the text. I separated each letter into its own text box which allowed me to manipulate the arrangment. Through this, I was able to place some letters on top of the scissors and some behind it. This creates the illusion that the word slice is being cut into. The color palette is based on the primary colors but I toned it down so that the colors wouldn't be as strong.
