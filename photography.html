Photography

Capturing the Beauty of Life

Photography taught me the importance of finding the best angle and editing photos. Through improving the fundamentals of photography, my Adobe Photoshop skills have also grown. My photography is more focused on the natural world as well as authenticity. I love photography because of the ability to capture a moment in time so that I can look back on it. The images I have featured on this page are some of my favorite photos that I've taken from trips and events. Every image captured opens a window to my perspective of the event and/or trip. I see photography as a window for others to peer into your life and to experience a slice of your experiences.
